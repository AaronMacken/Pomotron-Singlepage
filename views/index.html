      The Pomodorro technique...

      The pomodorro technique is a time management technique developed in the 1980s. The way it works is as follows...

      • Pick a task to work on
      • Determine a set time interval to work in, typically 25-30 minutes
      • Work diligently during the interval, then take a 5 minute break afterwards
      • After four of these iterations (or pomodorros), give yourself a longer break, around 30-45 minutes

      The Pomo-Tron-9000...

      The Pomo-Tron-9000 is an application designed to assist you in using this technique.


      Not only can you track your number of pomodorros completed, but you can also track how accurate you are in estimating how long a certain task will take.


      By doing this, you will be able to better plan out your daily activities and have a more productive day overall!
